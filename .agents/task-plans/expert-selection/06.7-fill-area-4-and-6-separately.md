## Evidence requirements

Every newly recommended candidate must satisfy all three:

1. Direct practitioner proof: a source clearly identifies the person, role, and direct operating, advisory, founder, or practitioner experience.
2. Dated original material: a dated first-party post, video, podcast appearance, article, or other original public material attributable to the individual.
3. Area-specific technical proof: evidence supports the exact area being filled, not only broad sales, GTM, training, company, or product positioning.

A company, product, biography, or generic training page may provide context but cannot be the sole proof for a recommended candidate.

Do not call a candidate `Deep screened` unless all three requirements are satisfied.
